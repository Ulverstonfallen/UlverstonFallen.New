World War Two Roll of Honour

When the World War 2 menu item is clicked, this page is displayed, and a sub menu appears. This page is the Roll of Honour page for the World War Two fallen.

The sub menu has two items:

  • Roll of Honour lists the fallen of World War Two.

  • Map shows where the fallen of World War Two lived.

The menu was done this way because it was easy for me to implement. It can, and probably will change. This is only an ideas website.

Whenever one of these pages is displayed, the header title changes to Ulverston Fallen: World War Two.

A map page is optional, if there is no map page, there will be no sub menu as there are no other World War Two pages.

This page will work in the same way as the World War One version.
