Tyne Cot Memorial

Published by Paul Chappell, 12th November, 2018.

The names of 10 men of Ulverston are recorded at Tyne Cot, one of the four memorials to the missing in Flanders, located near the Belgian city of Ypres (or Ieper). Many of the names inscribed on this memorial were soldiers lost during the Battle of Passchendaele in the Great War.

The Commonwealth War Graves Commission website also has information on the Memorial.
