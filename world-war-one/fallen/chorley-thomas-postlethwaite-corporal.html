Corporal Thomas Postlethwaite Chorley

Service Number: 10254

Battalion: 2nd Battalion

Regiment: East Lancashire Regiment

Date Of Death: 25 Oct 1916

Age: 25

Address: 43 Quebec Street, Ulverston

Memorial: Thiepval; Pier and Face 6 C.

Location: France; Somme

The Commonwealth War Graves Commission website also has information regarding this person

Son of John Chorley and Elizabeth Ann Gardner (formerly Chorley), 43 Quebec St, Ulverston, Lancs.

Thomas’ next of kin was listed as Mr W G Postlethwaite (grandfather) of 24 Quay Street, Ulverston.

Mr W G Postlethwaite was reported in the Barrow News in 1916 as having 3 sons and 3 grandsons serving in the army.

Thomas was injured at Mons but returned to service and survived until being killed in France in 1916.

A cross was laid for Thomas Postlethwaite Chorley at Thiepval on 8th August 2018
