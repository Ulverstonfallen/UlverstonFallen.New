Private Thomas Edward Atkinson

Service Number: 46377

Battalion: 13th Battalion

Regiment: Royal Welsh Fusiliers

Date Of Death: 28 August 1918

Age: 35

Cemetry: Delville Wood, Longueval; XXII. L. 6.

Location: France; Somme

The Commonwealth War Graves Commission website also has information regarding this person

Son of Joseph and Annie Atkinson; husband of Ada Atkinson of 18 Troutbeck Bridge, Windermere.

Thomas was 33 years old and a gardener by trade on enlistment; he lived at Aynsome Cottages in Cartmel.

There are two dates of enlistment recorded on Thomas’ attestation certificate, 2 December 1915 and 7 June 1916. Other military records show his reckonable service started on 7 June 1916.

A cross was laid for Thomas Edward Atkinson at Delville Wood Cemetery on 7 August 2018. Thomas Edward Atkinson Grave
