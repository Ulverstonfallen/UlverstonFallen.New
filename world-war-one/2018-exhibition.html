2018 Exhibition

World War One Exhibition at the Ulverston Library, November 2018.

In 2018 an exhibition was held at the Ulverston library, to commemorate the people of Ulverston who died of their injuries while fighting in World War One. Three of the charts from the exhibition can be viewed as PDF documents.

Great War Battles lists the battles that took place during the first world war and shows the number of fallen on each day of the war.

Great Pilgrimage 90 gives details of the great pilgrimage of 2018, which took place 90 years after the first one.

The King’s Own (Royal Lancaster Regiment) gives details of the regiment.
