Ulverston Fallen

This is the home (or index) page, it probably needs to be re-written, especially if the World War Two Fallen are added.

I’ve moved the menu; it is now below the header image (no problem putting it back). The Contact menu item has been moved to the footer to reduce the number of items in the header menu. Gallery has been renamed Blog. A new item named Events has been added. Roll of Honour and Map have been combined into a new sub menu, displayed when World War 1 is clicked. I’ve also added a new menu item called World War 2, which also displays a sub menu.

I used 1 and 2, rather than One and Two to keep the menu small. Using names meant the menu does not fit on a single line at very small screen sizes.

The title at the top of the header image has been changed to Ulverston Fallen. Ulverston Fallen: World War One and Ulverston Fallen: World War Two are only displayed when the World War 1 and 2 menu items are clicked.

Further explanations are on the various pages of the website.
