Dar es Salaam Cemetery

Tags: Dar-es-Salaam Cemetery

1 January 2023

Whilst working for the Commonwealth War Graves Commission, Ulverston stone mason, David Lamb, laid crosses and flowers on the graves of two of Ulverston’s fallen when he visited Dar-Es-Salaam War Cemetery in January 2023.

The Commonwealth War Graves Commission website also has information on the cemetery.
